Why Computer Science?

I've never really had a for sure answer to what career I wanted. I knew that I want a career with computers, but I've always

switched between engineering, criminology, and computer science. When I came to Northwest, I was originally a Cybersecurity major,

I switched to a Criminology major with a minor in Cybersecurity, and now I'm majoring in Computer Science. For a long time, I wanted

to be a criminal technical analyst, and after taking two computer classes this semester, I've learned that I really enjoy coding,

so now I'm interested in doing something with that.
